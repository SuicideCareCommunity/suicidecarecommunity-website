Our mission

To ease the financial burden on families after the loss of a loved one to suicide — so they can focus on healing and recovery during their time of grief.

Families who lose someone to suicide often face sudden costs — funeral expenses, lost income, unexpected bills — at the most painful moment of their lives, and frequently with less support than other forms of loss. We exist to lift some of that weight.
Who we help

Direct relief for the immediate family of someone lost to suicide.

Parent

Help for parents enduring the unimaginable loss of a child.

Child

Relief for children and dependents who have lost a parent.

Sibling

Assistance for siblings carrying the weight of a loss.

Legal Guardian

Support for guardians caring for those left behind.

Qualifying loss

The death of an immediate family member by suicide.

Application window

Within 24 months of the loss.

How it works

A simple, private path from reaching out to relief.

1

Reach out

Apply online or email us. Tell us a little about what you're facing.

2

We verify

We confirm eligibility and review any documentation with care.

3

We match support

Approved help is matched to your family's specific needs.

4

Relief arrives

Funds are paid quickly — directly to providers or to you.
